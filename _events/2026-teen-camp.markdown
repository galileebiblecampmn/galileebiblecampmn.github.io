#### What to Bring

- Bible, pencil/pen, notebook
- Sleeping bag or single bunk bedding
- Towels, soap, toothpaste, toothbrush
- Insect repellent, tennis shoes, socks, swimsuit, waterproof sunscreen
- Extra clothes and a jacket
- **Optional (teens):** A bike for trail riding
- **Optional (teens):** Camp-appropriate instruments for worship

#### What Not to Bring

- Radios, TVs, DVD players, or electronic games
- Fireworks, weapons, illegal substances
- Food, drinks, alcohol, energy drinks, or tobacco
- Bikinis or immodest swimwear; immodest or inappropriate clothing

#### Responsibilities

- Keep your living area clean and orderly.
- Do not leave camp without permission of the Camp Dean.
- Camp visitors who use Lake Bronson State Park facilities must have a Minnesota Park sticker or day pass.
- All teens must register their vehicles and deposit their keys with the camp deans.
