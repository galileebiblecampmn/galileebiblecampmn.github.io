What to Bring:
Bring your Bible, pencil or pen, a notebook, a
sleeping bag or single bunk bedding, towels,
soap, toothpaste and toothbrush, insect repellent,
tennis shoes and socks, swimsuit, waterproof
sunscreen, extra clothes, and jacket.
NEW this year for TEENS: you can bring a bike
for trail riding. You are also invited to bring campworthy instruments to help lead contemporary
worship.
What not to bring:
Do not bring radios, TV’s, DVD players, or personal
electronic games. Apparel for daily wear and
especially the evening services, should be modest
and appropriate for worshiping God. No bikinis,
or immodest swim wear or sun-wear. No
fireworks, weapons, or illegal substances. No food
or drinks, alcoholic drinks, energy drinks or
tobacco etc.
All persons are responsible for cleanliness and
orderliness of the area in which they live each day.
No one is allowed to leave camp without
permission of the Camp Dean. Camp visitors who
wish to visit Lake Bronson State Park must have a
Minnesota Park sticker or a day pass issued when
they enter the park. All teens must register their
vehicles.
Location
Drive on Hwy 59 north from Karlstad to Lake
Bronson, MN. Take Co Rd #28 east into Lake
Bronson State Park. Stop at the park office to tell
them you’re going to Galilee, and they won’t
charge you. Turn left at the second left turn and
follow the paved road until you get to the sign
about 1 mile on your right.
