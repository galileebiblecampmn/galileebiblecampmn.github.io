Health Certificates
Law requires that any person under
18 who attends camp without a
parent or guardian must have a
health certificate, insurance number,
immunization record, and liability
release form filled out and submitted
to the camp. Complete this online
prior to arrival at camp.

Location
Drive on Hwy 59 north from Karlstad
to Lake Bronson, MN. Take Co Rd #28
east into Lake Bronson State Park.
Stop at the park office to tell them
you’re going to Galilee, and they
won’t charge you. Turn left at the
second left turn and follow the paved
road until you get to the sign about 1
mile on your right.
